# Aragorn - Ranger King

## Basic Information

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Full Name | Aragorn II, son of Arathorn |
| Age | 87 (appears ~45 due to Dunedain heritage) |
| Race/Species | Man (Dunedain) |
| Occupation | Ranger of the North, later King |
| Affiliation | Rangers of the North, Fellowship of the Ring |

## Physical Description

Tall and lean, weathered from years in the wild. Dark hair streaked with grey,
keen grey eyes that seem to pierce through deception. Bears himself with quiet
authority despite humble ranger's garb.

## Personality

### Traits
- Patient and wise beyond apparent years
- Humble despite royal lineage
- Fierce in battle, gentle in peace
- Deep sense of duty and honor

### Motivations
- Reclaim throne of Gondor
- Protect the weak and innocent
- Reunite with Arwen
- Defeat Sauron and restore peace

### Flaws
- Self-doubt about worthiness to be king
- Haunted by Isildur's failure
- Sometimes too cautious

## Background

Raised in Rivendell under the name Estel (Hope), unaware of his true heritage
until age 20. Spent decades ranging the wild lands, protecting the Shire and
northern kingdoms from evil. Served in Rohan and Gondor under assumed names.

## Relationships

- [[Arwen Undomiel]] - Beloved, betrothed
- [[Elrond]] - Foster father
- [[Gandalf]] - Mentor and ally
- [[Legolas]] - Close friend and companion
- [[Gimli]] - Unlikely friend
- [[Boromir]] - Complex relationship, respect earned

## Skills & Abilities

1. Master swordsman (trained by Elves)
2. Wilderness survival and tracking
3. Healing hands (Athelas/Kingsfoil)
4. Leadership and strategy
5. Linguistics (multiple languages)

## Equipment / Possessions

- Anduril (reforged Narsil) - The Flame of the West
- Ranger's cloak and gear
- Ring of Barahir (heirloom)
- Evenstar pendant (gift from Arwen)

## Character Arc

Begins story hiding identity, ends as High King of the Reunited Kingdom.
Journey is about accepting destiny and proving worthy of the throne through
deeds rather than lineage.
